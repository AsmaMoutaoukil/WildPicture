Exposures

FOAM : Feast for the Eyes - The story of Food in Photography

In December, Foam will present the exhibition Feast for the Eyes - The History of Food in Photography. This exhibition shows the rich history of culinary photography - not only in the visual arts, but also in commercial and scientific photography and photojournalism.

from December 21, 2018, to March 6, 2019

Border line, at the bend of the Jordan, Farida Hamak

Regard Sud gallery presents the exhibition Border line, at the bend of the Jordan Farida Hamak, whose practice is marked by his experiences as a war reporter at the Viva agency and at the agency Sipa Press, on the one hand, and as fashion editor of the Paris office of Al Khaleejiah-France, on the other hand.

From January 15, 2019, to March 9, 2019

International fair of the photo of bièvres

The fifty-sixth edition of the International Photo Fair in Bièvres June 1 and 2, 2019. Exhibitions, a second-hand market and conferences will be again from the party!

Traffic Festival, festival of the young european photography

The festival Circulation (s) is the project of Fetart, an association created in 2005 to promote young photographers and which currently has about fifty volunteers. Fully assembled and run by volunteers, the Fetart association and the Festival Circulation (s) are driven by a dynamic and a team spirit that show another face of contemporary art and creates a real dialogue with the public. Both public and demanding, the Circulation (s) festival brings together professionalism and passion.

From april 20, 2019, to June 30, 2019
